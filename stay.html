stay

The unit at kuriyana stay is built keeping comfort and modern adaptation of traditional techniques in mind. We took inspiration from the traditional "kath-khuni" method and added our take on it. Rooms at kuriyana stay have been built using Himalayan cedar also known as "deodar". Flooring is also done with natural stone, This makes rooms warm and comfortable inside with a scent that is unique to deodar.

We have specifically planned kuriyana stay experience for cozy family vocations, a lively get-together of friends or couples on a romantic getaway. The two room sets are spacious with large windows to let in natural light and give a sense of openness. Both rooms include king size beds and a set of table and chair so you can sip on your tea while watching a sunset. Both rooms have oil heaters and a fireplace to keep it warm.

Large windows all around you giving you a nice view of forests while you read a book or listen to your favourite music under a thick blanket.

food

the Indian cold climate. At kuriyana stay we serve simple and healthy home cooked food. A typical meal would consist of home grown pulses with rice and greens, chapatis, ghee and chutney. If you're non-vegetarian then our meat dishes will leave you licking your fingers after the meal beacuse of its flavourful and exotic appeal.

Some Interesting cuisines include chutney made from bhang seeds, cake made from a pulp of chuloo fruit & Jam made from viscous pulp of (Plum, Kafal, Honey and Hesar). Variety of cuisines is diverse and stimulating to say the least.
