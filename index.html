Kuriyana Stay

about

Kuriyana stay also offers two beautiful trekk routes for Bhadraj Temple & Jwala Devi Temple of Binog. Commute to kuriyana stay :-- From Kitabghar mussoorie : - ( 8.4 Kms ) From Dehradun railway station :- ( 39 Kms ) From Jolly Grant airport :- ( 42 Kms )

The region is inhabited by Jaunpuri community and is primary agrarian, seasons and harvest plays an important part in their lives. Dance and music is very core to their culture. For the food served at kuriyana stay we have taken inspiration from regional cuisines. We as a small start-up is aiming to build sustainable businesses for locals and providing them with jobs near their home to stop their migration.

About owner:- kuriyana stay has been built on a piece of land which belongs to the Rawat family, and they belongs to Ghandiyala village. Some of the members have moved to Dehradun for education and work. Kuriyana stay is managed by Sachin Rawat (youngest of the Rawat family), who have visited around 50+ countries beacause of his 10 years experince in Merchant Navy. He is very patriotic and always wanted to promote indian culture and saw huge potential of leisure tourism in India. He has many stories from his adventure and visits to different places worth listen to, over a cup of tea.
